We Are Chemical Engineers

Chemical engineers also strive to create better materials, which have improved properties, are more durable and last longer.

Chemical Engineering Society (ChES) is the student chapter of the chemical engineering department, SVNIT. It was institutionalised in by the batch of 2014 on January 16th, 2014. The faction provides a platform for the chemical engineering community of SVNIT where they can discuss, plan, execute and debug their problems among their peers. Events and seminars conducted by ChES focus on both the building the public understanding of technical issues and the individual curiosity of the enthusiasts.

ChES is affiliated to the American Institute of Chemical Engineering (AIChE).

Siphon is our annual departmental festival which aims at improving the public understanding of basic chemical engineering and chemistry. The other major initiative by ChES is Eureka! Eureka provides a platform for budding chemical engineers to come up with solutions to different industrial problems through research methodology. Other small events and talks on career and general awareness are organised throughout the year. Undergraduate students of the second year and beyond lead the student chapter. Pupils need to clear the interview round to be a part of the community.

About SVNIT

Sardar Vallabhbhai National Institute of Technology, Surat is a public funded technical institute, established by the Government Of India in 1961. It is one of 30 National Institutes of Technology in India recognized by the Government of India as an Institute of National Importance.

Chemical Engineering department of SVNIT was established in 1995. More details can be found at SVNIT Website
